- using the software, in whole or in part, in a **product or service you sell,
  license, or monetize** (including ad-supported or "freemium" offerings);
- **hosting** the software, or a derivative, as a service for third parties
  (SaaS), whether paid or free-of-charge as part of a commercial offering;
- **reselling, sublicensing, or redistributing** the software as part of a
  commercial product;
- **paid integrations** — connecting it to a paid bot, paid module, paid API, or
  any subscription offering (for example, a paid module inside a commercial
  Telegram bot);
- internal use by a **for-profit organization** in connection with its
  commercial operations, beyond genuine evaluation, research, or experimentation;
- any use with an **anticipated commercial application.**
